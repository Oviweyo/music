About the Group

Music rooted in unity, growth, and positive energy.

Positive Minds is a music group based in Gqeberha, made up of artists originally from different towns across the Eastern Cape. The group consists of Siyabulela Swartbooi (Msawd) from Patensie, Sibusiso Jim (Soul B) from Alexandria (Emnyameni), and Mdunyelwa Mlungiseleli (Embonation) from Mdantsane.

The group was formed towards the end of 2018 with the goal of spreading positivity, unity, and powerful messages through music. In the same year, they released their debut single titled “Phola”, followed by a music video that was independently produced with support from a local creative in the northern areas of Port Elizabeth.

In 2019, Positive Minds released their second single, “Usandikhumbula”, featuring DJ Sk and Minollar. Later that year, they dropped their first EP titled “INDABA YAM”, marking an important milestone in their musical journey.

Over the years, the group has shared stages with well-known artists and DJs such as DJ Sk, Thembi Mona, T-Man, Cairo CPT, Flash Ikumnkani, and many others. They have also performed at major local events including Imibala Music Festival, Craddock In Colour Picnic, Motherwell Picnic, and several self-hosted events.

Positive Minds continues to grow as a collective, using music as a platform to inspire, connect communities, and deliver meaningful stories rooted in real experiences.
